# subscribe to
##### 词义
- 美：/ səbˈskraɪb tu /；
- #词性/v  订阅；加入，支持；赞同，支持
##### 例句
- Which journals does the library subscribe to ?
	- 图书馆订有哪些报刊？
- Maybe I oughtto subscribe to the Engineering Quarterly.
	- 也许我应该订阅《工程季刊》。
- If you subscribe to this newspaper, you'll get an extra magazine.
	- 如果你订阅这份报纸，你会得到一本额外的杂志。
