# look after
##### 词义
- 英：/ lʊk ˈɑːftə(r) /；美：/ lʊk ˈæftər /；
- #词性/v  照顾，照料；照看，监视
##### 例句
- I know the Lord will look after him.
	- 我知道上帝会照顾他的。
- It makes sense to look after yourself.
	- 照顾好你自己是明智的。
- Your baby depends on you to look after yourself properly while you are pregnant.
	- 你的宝宝靠你怀孕时好好照顾你自己。
