# noticeably
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  明显地；显著地
##### 例句
- Marks were noticeably higher for girls than for boys .
	- 女孩子的分数明显地高于男孩子。
- Her hand was shaking noticeably .
	- 很明显她的手在颤抖。
- The runners have noticeably quickened their pace .
	- 赛跑者明显加快了脚步。
