# nonflammable
##### 词义
- #词性/adj  不可燃；不易燃
##### 例句
- All products are made of distilled water , nonflammable .
	- 所有产品都是水性的，用蒸馏水制作而成。
- Product is nonflammable and does not support combustion .
	- 该产品为不可燃物不支持燃烧。
- Dry-type transformers are completely nonflammable , using organic material such as epoxy resin .
	- 干式变压器是完全不燃的，采用如环氧树脂这样的有机材料。
