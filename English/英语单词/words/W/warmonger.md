# warmonger
##### 词义
- 英：/ˈwɔːmʌŋɡə(r)/； 美：/ˈwɔːrmʌŋɡər/
- #词性/n  战争贩子；好战分子
##### 例句
- He was no warmonger but he did believe in nuclear weapons .
	- 他不是个战争贩子，但他相信核武器。
- The socialists were trying to smear Churchill as a warmonger .
	- 工党企图诽谤丘吉尔先生是战争贩子。
- In the eyes of warmongers , soldiers are nothing but cannon fodder .
	- 战争贩子们视士兵为炮灰。
