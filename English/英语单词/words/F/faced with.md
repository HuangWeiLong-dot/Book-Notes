# faced with
##### 词义
- 美：/ feɪst wɪð /；
- #词性/v  面临，面对
##### 例句
- We are faced with a difficult choice.
	- 我们面临着困难的抉择。
- They are faced with a mountain of bureaucracy.
	- 他们面临着一大堆的官僚制度。
- He was faced with a barrage of angry questions from the floor.
	- 他面临着听众一连串的愤怒质问。
