# from now on
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  从现在开始
##### 例句
- You should look out for yourself from now on.
	- 从现在起你应该多为自己着想。
- We're going to have to economize from now on.
	- 我们得从现在起节省开支。
- Perhaps it would be best not to see much of you from now on.
	- 或许从现在开始最好少和你见面。
