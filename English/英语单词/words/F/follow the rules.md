# follow the rules
##### 词义
- #词性/v  遵守规则
##### 例句
- It's necessary to follow the rules in any games.
	- 在任何游戏中，遵守规则都是必要的。
- No matter what you do, you have to follow the rules .
	- 无论你做什么，你都要遵守规则。
- To follow the rules , they are not allowed to smoke, drink wine, or speak rudely.
	- 为了遵守规则，他们不被允许吸烟、喝酒和说话粗鲁。
