# far from
##### 词义
- #词性/prep  远离，远非
##### 例句
- The restaurant is not far from here.
	- 饭店离这儿不远。
- Don't leave now: our task is far from complete!
	- 现在不要离开：我们的任务还远未完成！
- My stay at the hotel was far from satisfactory.
	- 我在酒店的住宿体验远非令人满意。
