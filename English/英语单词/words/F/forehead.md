# forehead
##### 词义
- 英：/ˈfɔːhed/； 美：/ˈfɔːrhed/
- #词性/n  前额；额头；额
##### 例句
- He has a round face with a high forehead .
	- 他圆脸，高额头。
- How did you get that bump on your forehead ?
	- 你额头上怎么起了个包？
- The stone struck her on the forehead .
	- 石头击中她的额头。
