- #词性/n  伪造；弄虚作假

- Any falsification of evidence will be investigated under the law .

- The Analysis of Falsification Criterion Based on the Truth of Marxism
	- 基于马克思主义真理观视角的证伪原则评析

	- 科技编辑证伪法的原则及特点
