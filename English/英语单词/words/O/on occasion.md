# on occasion
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  偶尔，有时候
##### 例句
- She had on occasion praised him extravagantly.
	- 她有时曾过分地表扬他。
- He has been known on occasion to lose his temper.
	- 大家都知道他有时会发脾气。
- It is, however, necessary on occasion to work outwith these hours.
	- 然而，有时有必要在这些时间之外工作。
