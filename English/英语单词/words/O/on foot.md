# on foot
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  步行
##### 例句
- I left my car in a lay-by and set off on foot.
	- 我把车留在路侧停车带，开始步行。
- I will pay for a hack. There is no need for you to return home on foot.
	- 我会花钱打辆出租车。你没有必要步行回家。
- Try going there on foot.
	- 试着步行去那儿。
