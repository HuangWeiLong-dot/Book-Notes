# as though
##### 词义
- #词性/conj  好像，仿佛
##### 例句
- You look as though you slept badly.
	- 你好像没睡好觉。
- It felt as though he had run a marathon.
	- 他感到好像跑了一个马拉松似的。
- It sounds as though you had a good time.
	- 听起来你好像过得挺愉快。
