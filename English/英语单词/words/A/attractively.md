# attractively
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  吸引人的；动人地，迷人地
##### 例句
- The room is arranged very attractively .
	- 这个房间布置得十分宜人。
- Dress neatly and attractively in an outfit appropriate to the job
	- 着装要整洁美观，适合于这份工作。
- Set out the cakes attractively , using lacy doilies .
	- 铺上网眼垫纸，将蛋糕摆得诱人些。
