# as soon as
##### 词义
- 美：/ æz suːn æz /；
- #词性/conj  一……就……
##### 例句
- I'll be back as soon as I can.
	- 我会尽快回来。
- I will come as soon as I possibly can.
	- 我会尽快赶来的。
- We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
	- 我们将尽快再跟你联系。
