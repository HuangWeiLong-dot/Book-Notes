# assent
##### 词义
- 英：/əˈsent/； 美：/əˈsent/
- #词性/n  同意；赞成
- #词性/v  同意，赞成(要求、想法或建议)
##### 例句
- Nobody would assent to the terms they proposed .
	- 谁也不会同意他们提出的条件。
- The director has given her assent to the proposals .
	- 负责人已表示同意提案。
- The bill passed in Parliament has now received the Royal Assent .
	- 议会通过的法案已获御准。
