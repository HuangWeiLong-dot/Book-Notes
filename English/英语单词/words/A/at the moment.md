# at the moment
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  现在，当前时间
##### 例句
- Business is looking shaky at the moment.
	- 从目前看，业务举步维艰。
- I am really enjoying myself at the moment.
	- 我此刻很是自得其乐。
- I'm off coffee at the moment.
	- 我暂时不再喜欢咖啡。
