# arson
##### 词义
- 英：/ˈɑːsn/； 美：/ˈɑːrsn/
- #词性/n  纵火(罪)；放火(罪)
##### 例句
- Seven men , all from Bristol , admitted conspiracy to commit arson
	- 7个人都来自布里斯托尔，他们承认了合谋纵火。
- The store burned down , and the police suspected arson .
	- 商店被烧毁了，警方怀疑有人纵火。
- They vented their anger by carrying out arson attacks .
	- 他们四处纵火以泄愤。
