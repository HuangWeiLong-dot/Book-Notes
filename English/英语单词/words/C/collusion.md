# collusion
##### 词义
- 英：/kəˈluːʒn/； 美：/kəˈluːʒn/
- #词性/n  勾结；串通；密谋
##### 例句
- The police were corrupt and were operating in collusion with the drug dealers .
	- 警察腐败，与那伙毒品贩子内外勾结。
- They act in collusion to control the market .
	- 他们勾结起来控制市场。
- There was collusion between the two witnesses .
	- 两个证人串通作伪证。
