# continually
##### 词义
- 英：/kənˈtɪnjʊəli/； 美：/kənˈtɪnjuəli/
- #词性/adv  不断地；不停地；持续地
##### 例句
- The other kids continually taunted him about his size .
	- 其他孩子不断地耻笑他的个头儿。
- The horses were continually pestered by flies .
	- 马不断地被苍蝇叮咬。
- She goes on at him continually .
	- 她老是责备他。
