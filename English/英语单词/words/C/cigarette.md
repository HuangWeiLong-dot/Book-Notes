# cigarette
##### 词义

- #词性/n  香烟；卷烟；纸烟
##### 例句
- There was no light except for the occasional glow of a cigarette .
	- 除偶尔有香烟的微弱红光外没有一点亮光。
- A cigarette set the dry grass alight .
	- 一支香烟把干草点燃了。
- The price of cigarettes is set to rise again .
	- 香烟又要涨价。
