# cast down
##### 词义
- 美：/ kæst daʊn /；
- #词性/v  沮丧的
##### 例句
- She was cast down by the news of her friend's illness.
	- 她因朋友生病的消息而感到沮丧。
- The failure of the project left him feeling cast down.
	- 项目失败让他感到沮丧。
