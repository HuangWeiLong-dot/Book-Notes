# plentifully
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  丰富地；大量地；充足地
##### 例句
- Evidence is plentifully available .
	- 证据俯拾即是。
- She kept them plentifully supplied with gossip .
	- 她不断向他们散播大量的流言飞语。
- The visitors were plentifully supplied with food and drink .
	- 给来宾准备了丰富的食物和饮料。
