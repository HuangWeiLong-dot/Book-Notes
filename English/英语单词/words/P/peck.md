# peck

- #词性/n  啄；匆匆的吻
##### 例句
- Newly hatched chicks can walk and peck .
	- 刚孵育出来的小鸡就会走、会啄食。
- The birds of night peck at the first stars
	- 夜晚的鸟群啄食第一阵闪烁的群星
- New Zealand is at the top of the pecking order of rugby nations .
	- 新西兰在橄榄球国家中首屈一指。
