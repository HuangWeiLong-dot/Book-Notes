# make a decision
##### 词义
- 美：/ meɪk ə dɪˈsɪʒn /；
- #词性/v  做决定
##### 例句
- We can't make a decision based on hearsay and guesswork.
	- 我们不能根据传言和猜测作决定。
- Check out the lie of the land before you make a decision .
	- 要摸清情况后再作决定。
- I've asked to see all the facts and figures before I make a decision .
	- 我已要求在看到所有的确切信息后再作决定。
