# make an effort
##### 词义
- #词性/v  努力，作出努力
##### 例句
- I've no use for people who don't make an effort .
	- 我厌恶那些不努力的人。
- I make an effort to be on time when I meet my friends.
	- 当我和朋友见面时，我会努力做到准时。
- France has promised to make an effort to rebuild Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris.
	- 法国承诺努力重建巴黎圣母院。
