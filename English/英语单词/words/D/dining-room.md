# dining-room
##### 词义
- 英：/ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm/； 美：/ˈdaɪnɪŋ ruːm/
- #词性/n  (家里或宾馆的)饭厅，餐厅
##### 例句
- The dining-room communicates with the kitchen .
	- 饭厅与厨房相通。
- There is a small dining-room in our school .
	- 我们学校有一个小饭厅。
- A cold buffet had been laid out in the dining-room .
	- 餐厅里已经摆好了自助冷食。
