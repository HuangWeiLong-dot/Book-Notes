# dillydally
##### 词义
- #词性/v  磨蹭；磨磨蹭蹭；浪费时间
##### 例句
- Students had no time to dillydally in the school cafeteria .
	- 学生们没时间在学校的自助食堂磨蹭。
- Don 't dillydally & just pack your things and then we can go !
	- 别磨蹭了&把你的东西打包，然后我们就能走了。
- Don 't dillydally . It 's time for going to school .
	- 别蘑菇了，该去上学了。
