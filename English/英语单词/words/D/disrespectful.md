# disrespectful
##### 词义
- 英：/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfl/； 美：/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfl/
- #词性/adj  不尊重的；无礼的
##### 例句
- Some people said he had been disrespectful to the President in his last speech .
	- 有些人说他在最近一次讲话中对总统不尊重。
- They shouldn 't treat their mother in this disrespectful way .
	- 他们不能这样无礼地对待亲生母亲。
- It would be disrespectful to decline .
	- 却之不恭。
