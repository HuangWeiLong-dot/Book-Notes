# distinctly
##### 词义
- 英：/dɪsˈtɪŋktli/； 美：/dɪˈstɪŋktli/
- #词性/adv  明显地；显然
##### 例句
- The marriage had been distinctly dodgy for a long time .
	- 这桩婚姻长期以来明显不和美。
- The prospect of studying for another five years was distinctly unappealing .
	- 未来还需要再学五年，真是让人厌烦。
- He could remember everything very distinctly .
	- 他什么事都能记得清清楚楚。
