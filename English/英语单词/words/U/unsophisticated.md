# unsophisticated
##### 词义
- 英：/ˌʌnsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/； 美：/ˌʌnsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/
- #词性/adj  简单的；单纯的；朴素的；不复杂的；不谙世故的；基本的；涉世不深的；阅历浅的
##### 例句
- Although St Lucia trades on its relatively unsophisticated image , it is not bereft of celebrity glamour .
	- 尽管圣卢西亚利用了其相对朴素的形象，但它并没有丧失其名人魅力。
- Madoff himself described his investment business as an unsophisticated " Ponzi scheme ," according to investigators who interviewed him .
	- 据调查员说，Madoff将他的投资生意描述成单纯的旁氏骗局。
- This basic utilitarian model gives a relatively unsophisticated account of human behaviour
	- 这个实用的基础模型较为简略地解释了人类的行为。
