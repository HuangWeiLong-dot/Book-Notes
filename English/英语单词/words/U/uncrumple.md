# uncrumple
##### 词义
- #词性/v  使回复到平整状态；去掉……的皱纹，弄平
##### 例句
- Eventually , the metal will crumple and uncrumple , totally unaided , in response to changes in temperature and without any sign of metal fatigue .
	- 最终，它会自动地随温度变化而弯曲和恢复这些形状，并且没有任何疲劳迹象。
