# in amazement
##### 词义
- 美：/ ɪn əˈmeɪzmənt /；
- #词性/adv  惊讶地
##### 例句
- I stared at her in amazement .
	- 我惊讶地盯着她。
- Heidi looked up to him in amazement .
	- 海蒂惊讶地抬头看着他。
- Clara, having just a moment ago opened her eyes, looked about her in amazement .
	- 克拉拉刚刚睁开眼睛，惊讶地环顾四周。
