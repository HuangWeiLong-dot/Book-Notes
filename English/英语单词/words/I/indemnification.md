# indemnification
##### 词义
- 英：/ɪnˌdɛmnɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/； 美：/ɪnˌdɛmnəfɪˈkeɪʃən/
- #词性/n  赔偿；赔偿，补偿；保护
##### 例句
- Three Chinese enterprises receive indemnification from the First National Bank of America
	- 中国三家企业获美国第一国民银行赔偿
- If loss is sustained , the other party may also claim indemnification .
	- 如有损失，并得请求赔偿。
- A research on the lowest rural life indemnification Standard of China
	- 中国农村最低生活保障标准研究
