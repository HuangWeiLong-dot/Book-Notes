# intention
##### 词义
- 英：/ɪnˈtenʃn/； 美：/ɪnˈtenʃn/
- #词性/n  意图；打算；目的；计划
##### 例句
- He has already stated his intention to run for election .
	- 他已声明打算参加竞选。
- He left England with the intention of travelling in Africa .
	- 他离开英格兰，打算去非洲旅行。
- She 's full of good intentions but they rarely work out .
	- 她虽然处处出于善意，却往往事与愿违。
