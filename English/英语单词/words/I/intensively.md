# intensively
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  强烈地； 集中地
##### 例句
- The river valley is intensively cropped .
	- 河谷里种满了庄稼。
- The calves are grazed intensively during their first season .
	- 小牛在生长的第一季里集中喂养
- The quality of education and teaching is concerned intensively .
	- 教育教学质量引起社会广泛关注。
