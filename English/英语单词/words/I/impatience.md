# impatience
##### 词义
- 英：/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/； 美：/ɪmˈpeɪʃəns/
- #词性/n  不耐烦；急躁；无耐心
##### 例句
- She was bursting with impatience to tell me the news .
	- 她迫不及待地要告诉我这个消息。
- He waited for her arrival in a fever of impatience .
	- 他焦急不安地等待她的到来。
- There is a certain impatience among some of the soldiers
	- 有些士兵有点儿不耐烦。
