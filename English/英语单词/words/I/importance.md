- #词性/n  重要；重要性；重大
##### 例句
- Number the car 's features from 1 to 10 according to importance .
	- 将车的特征从1到10编号按重要性一一列出。
- State your reasons in order of importance .
	- 按重要性顺序陈述你的理由。
- Experience is what matters ─ age is of secondary importance .
	- 重要的是经验——年龄是次要的。
