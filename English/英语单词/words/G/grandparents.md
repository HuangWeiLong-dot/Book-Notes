# grandparents

- #词性/n  祖父母；祖父；祖母；外祖父；外祖母
##### 例句
- The children are staying with their grandparents .
	- 孩子们与祖父母住在一起。
- My grandparents emigrated to Canada to try their luck there .
	- 我的祖父母移民到加拿大去碰碰运气。
- As a small boy he had spent most of his time with his grandparents .
	- 他幼年时多半时间是跟爷爷奶奶在一起。
