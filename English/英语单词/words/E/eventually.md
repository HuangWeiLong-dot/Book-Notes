# eventually
##### 词义
- 英：/ɪˈventʃuəli/； 美：/ɪˈventʃuəli/
- #词性/adv  最后；终于
##### 例句
- Eventually the search was called off .
	- 搜查最后被取消了。
- I 'll get round to mending it eventually .
	- 我最后会抽出时间来修理它的。
- Eventually , the new regulations will work to our advantage .
	- 新规章制度最终将对我们有利。
