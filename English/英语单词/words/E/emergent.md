- #词性/adj  新兴的；处于发展初期的

- Bioengineering is an emergent branch of learning .
	- 生物工程是一门新兴的学问。
- Emergent democracies created markets that were ripe for exploitation .
	- 新兴的民主国家创造了可供开拓的成熟市场。
