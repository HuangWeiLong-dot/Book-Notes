# telephone number
##### 词义
- 英：/ ˈtelɪfəʊn nʌmbə(r) /；
- #词性/n  电话号码
##### 例句
- Her telephone number differs from mine by one digit.
	- 她的电话号码跟我的差一个数字。
- Mr. Marra, whose New York telephone number is ex-directory, could not be contacted yesterday.
	- 马拉先生在纽约的电话号码没列入电话薄，昨天联系不上他。
- What's your telephone number ?
	- 你电话号码是多少？
