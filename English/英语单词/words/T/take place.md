# take place
##### 词义
- 美：/ teɪk pleɪs /；
- #词性/v  发生、举行
##### 例句
- She wanted Randy's wedding to take place quickly.
	- 她想让兰迪的婚礼马上举行。
- The meeting should take place on the fifth of May.
	- 会议应于5月5日举行。
- Elections take place every year.
	- 每年都进行各项选举。
