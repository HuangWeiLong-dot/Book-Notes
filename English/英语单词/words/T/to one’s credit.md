# to one’s credit
##### 词义
- #词性/adv  值得赞扬
##### 例句
- To his credit, he is honest about the problems the business faces.
	- 值得赞扬的是，他对企业面临的问题很诚实。
- To her great credit, she admitted she was wrong.
	- 值得赞扬的是，她承认自己错了。
- To the team's credit, they never stopped trying even after multiple defeats.
	- 值得赞扬的是，球队在多次失利后也从未停止过尝试。
