# have a cold
##### 词义
- #词性/v  感冒
##### 例句
- Whenever I have a cold I get a nosebleed.
	- 每次我一感冒都会流鼻血。
- Two students are absent because they have a cold .
	- 两个学生因为感冒缺席了。
- I have a cold and a fever.
	- 我不仅感冒还发烧了。
