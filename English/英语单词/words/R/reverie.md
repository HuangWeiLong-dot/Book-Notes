# reverie

- 英：/ˈrevəri/； 美：/ˈrevəri/
- #词性/n  幻想；梦想；白日梦
##### 例句
- She was jolted out of her reverie as the door opened .
	- 门一开就把她从幻想中惊醒。
- The announcer 's voice brought Holden out of his reverie .
	- 播音员的声音将霍尔登从幻想中惊醒。
- The song took me into a reverie of the grasslands .
	- 歌声把我带入对草原的冥想。
