# reminisce

- 英：/ˌremɪˈnɪs/； 美：/ˌremɪˈnɪs/
- #词性/vi  回忆；追忆；缅怀(昔日的快乐时光)
##### 例句
- He would often reminisce about his first experience of the sea .
	- 他经常回忆他的第一次航海经历。
- But no one likes to reminisce about the old slave days .
	- 没人愿意回忆旧的奴隶时代
- We spent a happy evening reminiscing about the past .
	- 我们一晚上回忆往事，感到很愉快。
