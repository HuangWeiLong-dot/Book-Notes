# river

- 英：/ˈrɪvə(r)/； 美：/ˈrɪvər/
- #词性/n  河；江；(液体)涌流
##### 例句
- The university is on the left bank of the river .
	- 大学在河的左岸。
- I could sit and watch the river all day long .
	- 我可以整天坐在这里看那条河。
- The general had to get his troops across the river .
	- 将军必须让部队过河。
