# vendetta
##### 词义
- 英：/venˈdetə/； 美：/venˈdetə/
- #词性/n  仇杀；宿怨；家族世仇；长期不和；团伙仇杀
##### 例句
- His murder was a vendetta killing .
	- 他的被害是一起仇杀。
- Of making this investigation seem like a personal vendetta .
	- 把这次调查搞得跟私人仇杀一样。
- He has accused the media of pursuing a vendetta against him .
	- 他指责媒体长期跟他过不去。
##### 形态
- #形态/word_pl vendettas
