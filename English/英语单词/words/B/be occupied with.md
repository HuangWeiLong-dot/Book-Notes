# be occupied with
##### 词义
- #词性/v  忙于做，专心于；忙碌
##### 例句
- Be occupied with your life.
	- 全身心的投入你的生活。
- It must not be occupied with problems, with worries, with speculations.
	- 你的心一定不能被问题、烦恼及臆测所占据。
- If the rabble continues to be occupied with you, simply stop reading that drivel.
	- 如果这群乌合之众继续纠缠你，就别读那些废话。
