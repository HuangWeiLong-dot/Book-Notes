- 英：/beɪn/； 美：/beɪn/

- The neighbours ' kids are the bane of my life .

- Spots can be the bane of a teenager 's life .
	- 粉刺会给青少年带来无尽的烦恼。
- Rain is the bane of holiday-makers
	- 雨天令度假者头疼。
