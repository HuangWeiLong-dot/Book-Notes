# build on
##### 词义
- #词性/v  进一步发展或扩展
##### 例句
- They're going to build on the site of the old power station.
	- 他们要在老发电站那里盖房子。
- Kindness can build on itself as much as violence can.
	- 善良可以像暴力一样建立在自身之上。
- If you can't make a decision, build on other people's suggestions.
	- 如果你不能自己做出决定，就参考别人的建议吧。
